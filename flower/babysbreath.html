BABY'S-BREATH

Baby’s Breath (Gypsophila) is a delicate and charming flower known for its airy clusters of tiny white or pink blossoms. Often used as a filler in floral arrangements, Baby’s Breath symbolizes purity, everlasting love, and sincerity, making it a popular choice for weddings and romantic bouquets. Its light and cloud-like appearance adds a soft, elegant touch to any floral design.
